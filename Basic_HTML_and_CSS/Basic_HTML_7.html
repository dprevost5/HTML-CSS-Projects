Basic Massage Techniques

Effleurage

Effleurage, a French word meaning "to skim" or "to touch lightly on", is a series of massage strokes
used in Swedish massage to warm up the muscle before deep tissue work using petrissage. This is a soothing,
stroking movement used at the beginning and the end of the facial and/or body massage. ©Wikipedia

Petrissage

Petrissage is a massage technique that applies deep pressure to the underlying muscles. Kneading, wringing,
skin rolling and pick-up-and-squeeze are the petrissage movements. They are all performed with the padded palmar
surface of the hand, the surface of the finger and also the thumbs. ©Wikipedia
